About Us

We are a locally owned and operated Eastern North Carolina company, established in 2025. At Carolina Sass & Threads, we’re inspired to spread comedy and positivity in an otherwise tense and anger-filled world. Our tees bring coastal vibes, Southern charm, and a whole lot of sass to brighten your day!
